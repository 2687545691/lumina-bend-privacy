Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Slide lenses into adjacent empty cells to bend colored beams. Match each beam with the correct target color to complete the level.

Can I play offline?

Yes. Lumina Bend is a single-player puzzle game. Your level progress is stored locally on your device.

How does Game Center work?

If you are signed in to Game Center on your device, Lumina Bend can report achievements and leaderboard scores through Apple Game Center.

Where is my progress saved?

Completed levels, best moves, star ratings, and related records are saved locally on your device.

Privacy & Data

Lumina Bend does not require account registration. Gameplay progress is stored locally on your device.

Game Center authentication and account information are handled by Apple. Lumina Bend does not directly collect or manage your Apple ID or Game Center account information.

When you choose to save a share card image, the app requests permission to add images to your Photos library. The app does not read your photo library.
